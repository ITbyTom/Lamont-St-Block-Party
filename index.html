Lamont St. Block Party

It’s time to celebrate our corner of the world! We’re excited to invite all our neighbors to the Lamont St. Block Party on Thursday, June 19th. We’ll be closing the street between Brian and Garner from 5:00 to 8:30 PM to make plenty of room for food, fun, and catching up.

Please bring a dish to pass for our neighborhood potluck—we’ll provide the 'Party Zone' and the plates! Whether you’ve lived here for decades or just moved in, come grab a spot on the asphalt and enjoy a summer evening together. Just remember to bring your own chairs!
